What is Embroidery?

Embroidery includes applying thread and needle to fabrics in order to decorate them. This can include beads, sequins, etc, as an add on to the decorations.

Older Embroidery

Older embroidery can be done via hand or machine. People would make art in the older times using embroidery. As you can see in the picture above, most ancient royal clothes would contain embroidery to show their wealth.

Newer Embroidery

Embroidery has come a long way. Most people like to embroider by hand, but machines come in handy. A lot of the clothes you see contain some type of embroidering. Lots of people will add embroidery onto their own clothing to "spice it up"! It has become a hobby that lots of people have taken up.
